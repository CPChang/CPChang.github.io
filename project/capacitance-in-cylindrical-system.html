Capacitance in Cylindrical System

Date Wed, 04 Feb 2015 Tags 3D-IC / Signal Integrity / Computational Electromagnetic

Introduction

In 3D-IC, the extraction of equivalent circuit of TSV (Through Silicon Vias) is the most fundamental part. However, hundreds of thousands of vias can take very long time to simulate. By utilizing the Multipole Boundary Element Method1, the analytic Green's function is given, without numerical integration. Meanwhile, the basis number can be dramatically reduced, and thus reduced the time for matrix inversion, which usually be the most time-consuming part in BEM.

Result

Charge Distribution in Homogeneous System

One can easily shown the proximity effect between vias with the same or opposite polarity. Charge tends to crowd on the both side of vias with opposite polarity, while shrink away with the same one.
